## What data we collect and why

### Newsletter subscription

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your **email address** and record your consent. We use this to send you updates about Refuge Worldwide programming, events, and news.

Your email is stored and managed by **Mailchimp** (Intuit Inc.). You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any newsletter email.

**Legal basis:** Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))

---

### Show and artist submissions

When you submit a show proposal, we collect:

- Your name, email address, and phone number
- Artist and guest names, pronouns, and biographies
- Artist and show images
- Instagram handles and show genres
- Show details (date, time, type, description)

This information is stored in our content management system (Contentful). We use it solely to review, schedule, and publish your show.

**Legal basis:** Contractual necessity / Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(b) / (a))

---

### Workshop applications

When you apply for a workshop, we collect contact details (name, email, phone), pronouns, DJ experience, and — optionally — background and identity information you choose to share to support equitable access to our programming.

This data is stored in a private Google Sheets spreadsheet and used solely for the purpose of selecting and communicating with workshop participants. **All workshop application data is deleted at the end of the relevant workshop.**

**Legal basis:** Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))

---

### Studio bookings

Studio bookings are handled via **Cal.com**. When you make a booking, your data (name, email, and booking details) is processed by Cal.com. Please refer to [Cal.com's privacy policy](https://cal.com/privacy) for details on how they handle your data.

---

### Community feedback forms

Our community and residents feedback forms are hosted by **Tally.so**. Data submitted through these forms is processed by Tally. Please refer to [Tally's privacy policy](https://tally.so/privacy) for details.

---

### Website analytics

We use **Fathom Analytics** to understand how visitors use our website. Fathom is a privacy-first analytics tool — it does not use cookies, does not track you across websites, and does not store any personal data. The aggregate statistics it provides (such as page views) cannot be used to identify you.

---

### Audio and video playback

Our radio archive uses audio and video streams from **SoundCloud**, **Mixcloud**, and **YouTube**. When you play content through these embedded players, those platforms may collect data about your listening or viewing activity in accordance with their own privacy policies.

For live stream video, we use **Mux**. Mux may collect technical data (such as playback quality and device information) to deliver the stream.

---

### Community chat

Our website embeds a **Discord** community widget. If you interact with it, your data is processed by Discord Inc. See [Discord's privacy policy](https://discord.com/privacy) for details.

## Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** how we process your data
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (where processing is based on consent)
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at **hello@refugeworldwide.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority. In Germany, this is the **Berliner Beauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit** ([berlinerbeauftragte.de](https://www.berlinerbeauftragte.de)).
